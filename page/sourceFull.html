Certificates of Insurance
Once your move is booked, we can issue a certificate of insurance for your building. Check with building management to confirm exactly what they require. Most buildings ask for both a certificate holder and an additional insured, so include those details in your request. No extra fee for this service!
Parking Permits
Need a parking permit? Let us know in writing at least 5 business days before your move. We handle permits year-round for Boston moves (except moves August 1st thru September 1st). The cost is $255 per permit/location, which includes pulling the permit and posting signs 48 hours before your move.
Insurance Options
When planning your move, it's important to understand the coverage available to protect your belongings. 617 Boston Movers offers two options:
Option A: Basic Valuation
  • Included at no extra cost
  • Covers $0.60 per pound per damaged item, in accordance with Massachusetts law
  • Simple, straightforward protection
Option B: Replacement Coverage
  • Additional charge applies
  • Covers the current replacement value of your items
  • Reimbursement may be based on repair, restoration, or depreciated value
  • Provides broader protection for valuable or fragile items
NEED MORE COVERAGE??  We have plenty of other options - up to $100,000. Give us a call or send us an email and we will be happy to assist in adding the coverage you require.
If we do not receive your selection, Option A (Basic Valuation) will be automatically applied.
Terms & Conditions:
  • Any damage must be clearly shown in the presence of our movers and noted on the Bill of Lading.
  • All claims must be filed within 15 days from the moving date.

  • Valuables such as jewelry, family photos, documents, laptops, checks, money, keys, and remotes should be removed prior to the move - we have no liability for these items.
